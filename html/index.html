The Modern Way To Find Parking

Roadi is the first mobile parking solution that connects you to your local parking community.

HOW IT WORKS

MANAGE YOUR SCHEDULE AND TIME

Use Roadi's schedule feature to know exactly when it's time to head to your next appointment. Create your own events or find events around your neighborhood!

PARK ON YOUR TIME

Get your time back! Get up to date arrival times based on your location, traffic, and estimated parking time.

BECOME A PARKING GURU

Know the best places to find parking. Get real-time street parking availability when it matters the most.

IMPROVE YOUR PARKING COMMUNITY

Social media is a critical part of today's world, and parking is no different. Post alerts, get event updates, and share news about what's going on around you.
